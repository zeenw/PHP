Our Story

Mytrip was founded in October 2006 and landed on Nasdaq in May 2014. It is the first Chinese company in the US stock market focusing on online leisure travel.

Benefiting from the rapid growth ... of the online leisure travel market and extensive support from customers, Mytrip has been the No. 1 online leisure travel market share in Canada since the fourth quarter of 2015.

By the end of 2018, Mytrip had partnered with more than 16,500 travel service providers, providing consumers with more than 2.2 million packaged travel products such as group tours and self-help, as well as a wealth of individual travel products such as air tickets, hotels, and visas. As of March 2019, Mytrip has served more than 108 million trips, and received more than 6 million customer reviews. The overall product satisfaction rate reached 93%.
